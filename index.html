4D Vision System
Worked with Dr. Hao Jiang. We developed 4D algorithms, which use multiple calibrated RGBD cameras for multiple people tracking, action recognition and 3D body parts localization.
  • Quanzeng You and Hao Jiang, "Action4D: Online Action Recognition in the Crowd and Clutter", 2019 IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), Long Beach, CA, USA, 2019, pp. 11849-11858.
